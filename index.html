D&G Polishing

What We Do

  • French Polishing

    With 30 years of experience in the industry, the team at D&G Polishing have knowledge in restoring and polishing all types of wooden furniture. From removing small marks to completely restoring the piece, we have the knowledge to do this. Using the best products available we’re able to colour match any furniture and ensure your results will look amazing.

  • Furniture Restoration

    Furniture restoration is the ideal solution if you’re looking to refresh already existing furniture. With experience in upcycling furniture, we can help you to transform any piece into something you’ll love. From changing the colour to making small changes to the style.

  • Bespoke Furniture

    Working with local partners we’re able to commission all types of furniture that can be made to measure. We’ll work from start to finish directly with you to ensure the piece is perfect for all of your needs and appears exactly as you would like.
